This is the homepage of the birthday stats API, developed by Chase Traver.

To use me, put your birthday in YYYYMMDD format in the url after a slash, e.g. https://birthday-facts-api.herokuapp.com/19970715

You can also narrow your results from getting everything related to your birthday to one resource by putting the resource you want in a slash after your birthday, e.g. https://birthday-facts-api.herokuapp.com/19970715/chinesezodiac

You can also request multiple resources simultaneously by using & to chain them, e.g. https://birthday-facts-api.herokuapp.com/19970715/chinesezodiac&birthflowers

Source code can be found at this location: https://github.com/chasetraver/birthday-api
